Bhawarkua Footpath Initiative: A Vision for a Safer City

Is architectural proposal mein, humne Indore ke Bhawarkua Square par Bholaram Ustad Marg par ek bade mudde ko hal karne ka lakshya rakha hai. Yeh mudda vendors dwara public spaces aur footpaths par kabza karna hai, jisse traffic jam aur paidal yatriyon ke liye suraksha ka khatra utpann hota hai.

Hamara samadhan ek comprehensive streetscape design hai jo public spaces ko behtar sangathan aur buniyadi dhancha (infrastructure) ke saath sudharta hai. Mukhya features mein shamil hain designated vendor zones takay vendors paidal marg aur sadakon par atikraman na karein. Ismein aakarshak aur vyavasthit stalls bhi shamil hain jo overall design ke saath milte hain. Is initiative ka uddeshya green spaces aur outdoor seating areas ke saath aesthetic appeal ko bhi badhana hai.

Iske alawa, plan mein wide, unobstructed paidal yatri marg aur clear signage aur accessibility ramps bhi shamil hain, jisse sabhi nagrikon, vishesh roop se vikalang logon ke liye, surakshit aur aasan yatra sunischit ho. Yeh sampoorn drishtikon (holistic approach) Bhawarkua mein urban jivan ko phir se paribhashit karne ka wada karta hai, ise surakshit, adhik prabhavi aur kafi adhik anandamay banata hai.
